# 📚 15-Day Power BI DAX Learning Journey

Welcome to my 15-day Power BI DAX learning journey.

Over the course of 15 days, I explored essential DAX concepts by solving real-world business problems using a single retail sales dataset. Each day focused on a different business scenario, helping me understand not only the DAX syntax but also when and why to use specific functions.

Every topic was accompanied by a LinkedIn post and a short video walkthrough where I explained the business problem, the DAX solution, and the key learning.

# 🚀 Key Takeaway

This 15-day journey taught me that DAX is not about memorizing functions—it's about understanding business questions and knowing how to manipulate filter context to answer them.

Each function solved a specific business problem, and together they helped me build a reusable DAX measure layer capable of supporting interactive and executive-level Power BI dashboards.
